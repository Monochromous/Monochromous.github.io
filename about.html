About Blåhajs

Blåhajs is a soft toy shark sold at IKEA stores worldwide. The name "Blåhajs" is Swedish for "blue shark". The toy is designed to be soft and huggable, with a cute and friendly appearance that appeals to children and adults alike.

The Blåhajs toy comes in different sizes, from small keychain versions to large stuffed animals. It is made from polyester and can be machine-washed for easy cleaning. The toy is also safe for children of all ages, as it meets the safety standards set by IKEA and international regulations.

Blåhajs has become a popular item among IKEA customers, with many people collecting multiple versions of the toy or using it as a decorative item in their homes. The toy has even gained a cult following on social media, with fans sharing photos and videos of their Blåhajs in various settings and situations.
